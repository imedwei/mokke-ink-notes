# InkUp

A handwriting-to-text app for e-ink tablets, designed for a more intuitive writing experience.

Most note-taking apps on e-ink devices treat handwriting as an image or rely on clunky keyboard input. InkUp recognizes your handwriting in real time and renders clean, formatted text as you write — no buttons, no menus, just pen on paper.

![InkUp tutorial showing handwriting recognition and drawing areas](docs/screenshot.png)

## Features

- **Live handwriting recognition** — write naturally and see your text appear as you go
- **Drawing areas** — scribble then drag down to insert free-form drawing zones for diagrams and sketches
- **Gesture editing** — strike through words to delete them, drag lines up or down to insert or remove blank lines, scribble over drawing area strokes to erase them
- **Undo/redo** — draw horizontally then move up to undo, down to redo
- **Formatting** — underline a line for headings, draw a dash for list items, indent to start a new paragraph
- **Auto-scroll** — pause at the bottom of the screen and the page scrolls up automatically
- **Markdown export** — share your notes as clean markdown text, with drawings embedded as SVG
